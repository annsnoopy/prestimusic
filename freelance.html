Freelance

I've worked in the metropolitan area as a freelance club date musician for decades. I play keyboards and piano for weddings, private parties, corporate events, concerts, sing alongs, you name it. I also play in the Irish American and Greek American music scenes. Pretty versatile for an Italian American boy!
