John T. Prestianni

Composer, Vocal Coach, Teacher, Accompanist

I grew up in Valley Stream Long Island, home of Steve Buscemi and Fred Armisen, among others. In elementary school, I struggled with clarinet and French Horn but my life changed when my parents brought a piano into the house. I fell in love with the Beatles, Steely Dan, Irving Berlin, JS Bach, Beethoven and everything in between. Music was it for me and it's made me a nice living so I'm lucky. Currently I live in West Harlem, have 3 darling 'niblets' (nieces Josie & Vivi, and nephew Gregory) and this website is dedicated to the memory of my sweet kitty Sunday. Please contact me if your are so inclined! Thank for visiting!
